## 1. Schedule Summary

- Founder target ownership: `55%`
- Co-founder target ownership: `45%`
- Founder ownership is earned by milestone completion.
- Co-founder ownership is earned by staged capital commitment plus commercial and operational milestones.
- Each stage requires written sign-off by both founders.
- `55/45` is fully earned only after both founders complete and sign off Stage `3`.
- By launch, the intended position is Founder at `Stage 2` and Co-founder at `Stage 1`.
- Stage `1` runway is March 2026 through August 2026 with founder-first execution; additional part-time support is added when founder capacity is no longer sufficient after mutual discussion and with the decision recorded in writing.
- Founder Stage `1` part-time salary rate is fixed at `JPY 1,500/hour` and can be revised only at Stage `2` by written founder and co-founder approval.
- Co-founder Stage `2` should be completed within `120 days` after launch, and Co-founder Stage `3` should be completed within `12 months` after launch unless both founders agree otherwise in writing.

### Founder Milestone Notes

- The core MVP launch scope should remain aligned with the current product plan: Directory, Marketplace, POS support, and Group Purchase.
- Feature detail may be refined in writing, but the co-founder cannot retroactively expand the scope of a completed stage without mutual agreement.
- Commercial metrics do not replace the founder's delivery obligations, but they may be used as additional launch-readiness evidence if both founders agree.
- Stage `1` execution model is founder-first for March-August 2026; additional part-time support is added when founder capacity is no longer sufficient.
- Founder is intended to reach `Stage 2` at launch and complete the final `10%` through post-launch product and operating stability rather than at launch itself.

### Co-founder Milestone Notes

- Stage `1` funding is intended for development until launch; any unused amount remains as company reserve.
- Stage `1` staffing starts founder-only by default; if founder capacity is no longer sufficient, additional part-time support is added after mutual discussion and with the decision recorded in writing.
- Stage `1` founder part-time salary rate is fixed at `JPY 1,500/hour`; any rate change is handled at Stage `2` review by written approval of both founders.
- Stage `2` and `3` funding are intended for actual company operation and growth after launch.
- Capital commitment does not by itself override the need to complete the related operational and commercial milestone for the same stage.
- Stage `1` funding detail and budget assumptions should follow [[stage-1-use-of-funds]] unless the founders agree in writing to an updated budget.
- Co-founder is intended to be at `Stage 1` at launch, complete `Stage 2` within `120 days` after launch, and complete `Stage 3` within `12 months` after launch unless both founders agree otherwise in writing.

## 4. Sign-Off Rules

- Each stage is earned only after written confirmation by both founders.
- No partial earn-in applies unless both founders agree in writing.
- If a co-founder capital commitment stage is not funded, the corresponding co-founder ownership for that stage is not earned.
- If a founder milestone stage is not completed, the corresponding founder ownership for that stage is not earned.
- If Co-founder `Stage 2` or `Stage 3` is not completed within the agreed post-launch timing, the corresponding unearned co-founder equity remains unearned unless both founders agree otherwise in writing.
- Any equity tied to an uncompleted earn-in stage is not earned and does not transfer.

---

## 5. Full Earn-In Point (`55/45`)

- The ownership structure becomes fully earned at `55/45` only when:
1. Founder Stage `3` is completed and signed off.
2. Co-founder Stage `3` capital commitment and milestones are completed and signed off.
- Before this full earn-in point, each founder owns only the cumulative percentage already earned through completed stages.
- The remaining percentage stays unearned and does not transfer.
- Founder graduation and visa-status switch affect full-time employment transition timing, not whether previously completed earn-in stages remain earned.

---

## 6. Re-Scope Rule

- If the product roadmap changes materially, the founders must update this schedule in writing before using the new scope as a milestone test.
- If commercial conditions change materially, the founders must update the co-founder milestone evidence in writing before using the new standard for sign-off.
