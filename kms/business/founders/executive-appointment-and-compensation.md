## 1. Current Phase

- The Co-founder may hold the initial legal authority role needed for incorporation and early operations under the selected legal form.
- The Founder leads product and technical execution on a part-time basis until graduation.
- During Stage `1` (March-August 2026), the Founder may receive approved part-time salary under [[stage-1-use-of-funds]].
- Full-time CEO compensation does not begin until the CEO transition trigger is met.
- After graduation, the Founder is expected to switch to employee visa status in Halava before moving full-time.

---

## 2. CEO Transition Trigger

The Founder moves into the full-time CEO role when all of the following are met:

1. Halava is incorporated under the selected legal form (`KK` or `GK`).
2. Founder ownership documents are signed.
3. The Founder has graduated.
4. The Founder has switched to a visa status that permits full-time employee work for Halava.
5. The Founder can legally work full-time for Halava.
6. The MVP is live.
7. Halava has at least `10` active merchants or equivalent commercial validation.
8. Halava has at least `6 months` runway after founder compensation starts.

---

## 3. Appointment Structure

Once the transition trigger is met:

- the Founder becomes the intended long-term `CEO`
- the Founder starts full-time work as an employee in Halava
- the Founder should also receive the appropriate authority role under the selected legal form
- if `KK` is selected, target roles include `Director` or `Representative Director`
- if `GK` is selected, target roles include `Managing Member` or `Representative Member`
- the founders should document the authority transition in writing before or at the time of appointment

---

## 4. Compensation Principles

- Full-time founder compensation begins only after the transition trigger is met.
- Stage `1` part-time salary is a pre-transition operating expense and not a substitute for post-transition CEO compensation.
- Compensation remains separate from equity ownership.
- Compensation should be approved in writing by the founders under the governance rules.
- Stage `1` founder rate (`JPY 1,500/hour`) is fixed through August 2026 and may be revised only at Stage `2` by written founder and co-founder approval.
- Compensation should be reviewed at least every `6` months during the early stage.
- Ordinary business expenses should be reimbursed under a simple written expense policy.

---

## 5. Open Items for Legal and Tax Review

- whether Founder compensation should be structured as salary, executive remuneration, or another form permitted under the final Halava setup
- whether the Founder should hold `Director`/`Representative Director` (`KK`) or `Managing Member`/`Representative Member` (`GK`) after transition
- payroll, tax, and social insurance consequences once compensation begins
