## 1. Purpose

This document sets out the working agreement between the Founder and the Co-founder regarding ownership, roles, commitment, governance, and founder conduct.

---

## 2. Parties

- **Founder**: product, technical, and long-term operating lead
- **Co-founder**: incorporation, capital, finance/admin, and business execution lead

The target ownership structure is `Founder 55%` and `Co-founder 45%`, subject to the earn-in and vesting terms in the related documents.

---

## 3. Core Founder Commitments

### Founder

- own product scope, architecture, and implementation
- deliver the agreed milestone schedule
- act in the best interests of Halava
- work part-time until graduation within permitted legal scope
- for Stage `1` (March-August 2026), handle delivery first as the default staffing model
- if founder capacity is no longer sufficient during Stage `1`, additional part-time support is added after mutual discussion and recorded in writing
- for Stage `1` (March-August 2026), founder part-time salary is fixed at `JPY 1,500/hour` for approved working hours
- after graduation, complete visa-status transition required for full-time employee work in Halava
- prepare for long-term CEO transition

### Co-founder

- fund and support the staged capital commitment schedule
- support incorporation and early company operation
- support finance/admin setup and business execution
- discuss and agree any additional part-time hiring during Stage `1` (March-August 2026), with the decision recorded in writing
- during Stage `1`, co-founder compensation is equity earn-in based unless both founders agree otherwise in writing
- act in the best interests of Halava

---

## 4. Earn-In and Vesting

- Ownership is earned only under [[milestone-earn-in-schedule]].
- `55/45` is fully earned only after both founders complete and sign off Stage `3` under [[milestone-earn-in-schedule]].
- Earned ownership is subject to [[vesting-and-repurchase-terms]].
- Unearned equity does not transfer.

---

## 5. Decision-Making

The following matters require written approval by both founders or the agreed supermajority mechanism in the final legal documents:

- new equity issuance
- changes to founder ownership
- founder compensation changes
- any change to the Stage `1` founder part-time rate before Stage `2` review
- removal of a founder from executive authority
- debt above an agreed threshold
- sale of the company or major assets
- related-party transactions
- amendments affecting economics or control

---

## 6. Confidentiality and Conduct

- Both founders must keep Halava confidential information confidential.
- Both founders must disclose outside business interests that could affect Halava.
- Neither founder may use Halava funds, customer data, or business opportunities for personal or outside-business benefit without written approval.
- All Halava work product is governed by [[ip-assignment]].

---

## 7. Departure and Default

- Founder departure, removal, or default is governed by [[vesting-and-repurchase-terms]] and the final signed legal documents.
- Company access, source code, customer data, passwords, and records must be transferred back to Halava immediately on departure.
- Any founder leaving Halava must cooperate with a practical transition of responsibilities.

---

## 8. Review Rule

- This founder agreement should be reviewed at each earn-in stage.
- Any change to ownership, earn-in, authority, or compensation must be made in writing and approved by both founders.
