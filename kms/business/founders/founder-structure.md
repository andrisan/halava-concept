## 1. Proposal Summary

- Halava legal form is open and will be finalized as either `Kabushiki-Kaisha (KK)` or `Godo-Kaisha (GK)`.
- Target ownership after full earn-in is `Founder 55%` and `Co-founder 45%`.
- Ownership is earned by milestone completion and staged capital commitment, not by valuation.
- All earned equity remains subject to `4-year vesting` with a `1-year cliff`.
- Founder will work part-time until graduation, then switch to employee visa status in Halava for full-time work.
- The Co-founder may hold the initial legal representative role needed for incorporation and early operations under the selected legal form.
- The Founder is the intended long-term `CEO`.
- Stage `1` operating runway is planned from March 2026 through August 2026, with founder-first execution and additional part-time support added when founder capacity is no longer sufficient, after mutual discussion and with the decision recorded in writing.
- Founder may receive a fixed Stage `1` part-time salary (`JPY 1,500/hour`) for actual work, while co-founder return in Stage `1` is via equity earn-in tied to capital and execution milestones.

## 2. Target Ownership

| Role | Target Ownership | Earn-In Basis |
|------|------------------|---------------|
| **Founder** | `55%` | Product scope, technical architecture, feature implementation, and launch execution |
| **Co-founder** | `45%` | Incorporation, capital, finance/admin setup, and commercial execution |

Detailed stage definitions are set out in [[milestone-earn-in-schedule]].

---

## 3. Capital Commitment

| Stage | Co-founder Capital Commitment | Use of Funds |
|-------|-------------------------------|--------------|
| **1. Formation / Build** | `JPY 3M` | March-August build-to-launch runway; founder handles execution first; additional part-time is added when founder capacity is no longer sufficient after mutual discussion and with the decision recorded in writing; unused amount remains as company reserve |
| **2. Launch / Early Operations** | `+JPY 5M` | Initial company operations after launch |
| **3. Running Capital / Growth** | `+JPY 7M` | Ongoing company operations and growth |

Unless the founders later agree otherwise in writing, these staged commitments are treated as non-refundable founder funding tied to the earn-in schedule, not as valuation-based investment rounds.

Stage `1` budget details and cost assumptions are set out in [[stage-1-use-of-funds]].
If Stage `1` funds are not fully used by launch, the remaining balance stays with Halava as operating reserve.
Founder Stage `1` part-time salary rate remains fixed through August 2026 and is reviewed for adjustment only at Stage `2` with written approval by both founders.

### When `55/45` Is Fully Earned

- The target `55/45` structure is fully earned only when both founders complete Stage `3` and sign off in writing under [[milestone-earn-in-schedule]].
- Before both Stage `3` sign-offs, each founder owns only the percentage already earned by completed stages.
- Any remaining percentage is unearned and does not transfer until its stage is completed and signed off.
- Graduation and visa-status transition govern full-time role and compensation timing; they do not cancel equity already earned from completed stages.

## 4. Legal Form and Authority Structure

- Both founders should be treated as true founders from day 1.
- The final legal form (`KK` or `GK`) should be decided before incorporation.
- The initial legal authority may sit with the Co-founder for incorporation and early company operation.
- The Founder should not be positioned only as a future employee.

Authority roles by legal form:

- If `KK` is selected: `Director` and `Representative Director`
- If `GK` is selected: `Member`, `Managing Member`, and `Representative Member`

When the Founder moves into long-term operating control, the Founder should also hold the appropriate authority role under the selected legal form.

---

## 5. Role Allocation

### Founder

- Work part-time until graduation.
- For Stage `1` (March-August 2026), handle execution directly as the default staffing model.
- After graduation, switch to employee visa status in Halava and move to full-time work.
- Lead product vision and roadmap
- Design and build the core platform
- Own technical architecture and engineering execution
- Deliver the agreed product milestones under the earn-in schedule
- Transition into the full-time `CEO` role when the transition conditions are met

### Co-founder

- Support company formation and early operation in Japan
- Contribute capital and financial credibility
- Support finance, administration, and operational setup
- Discuss and agree any decision to add additional part-time staffing during Stage `1`, with the decision recorded in writing
- Open customer and merchant pipeline
- Support business execution, governance, and early commercial traction

Any related-party matters involving outside business interests must be disclosed and approved in accordance with [[company-form-and-governance]].

---

## 6. CEO Transition

- The Founder becomes the full-time CEO once the agreed transition trigger is met.
- Full-time founder compensation begins only after the transition and remains separate from equity.
- Stage `1` part-time salary before the transition is governed by [[stage-1-use-of-funds]].
- The Founder should also receive the appropriate authority role under the selected legal form when long-term operating control is intended.

The Founder moves into the full-time CEO role when all of the following are met:

1. Halava is incorporated under the selected legal form (`KK` or `GK`).
2. Founder ownership documents are signed.
3. The Founder has graduated.
4. The Founder has switched to a visa status that permits full-time employee work for Halava.
5. The Founder can legally work full-time for Halava.
6. The MVP is live.
7. Halava has at least `10` active merchants or equivalent commercial validation.
8. Halava has at least `6 months` runway after founder compensation starts.

Detailed transition and compensation terms are set out in [[executive-appointment-and-compensation]].

---

## 7. Governance

- All earned equity is subject to [[vesting-and-repurchase-terms]].
- All product, code, brand, and process IP must be assigned to Halava under [[ip-assignment]].
- Good leaver / bad leaver treatment must be defined in signed documents.
- Related-party transactions require prior written approval.

The following matters require both founders' approval or a defined supermajority rule:

- New equity issuance
- Changes to founder ownership
- Removal of a founder from executive authority
- Founder compensation changes
- Any change to the Stage `1` founder part-time rate before Stage `2` review
- Any decision to add additional part-time staffing during Stage `1` (March-August 2026), after mutual discussion and with the decision recorded in writing
- Debt above an agreed threshold
- Sale of the company or major assets
- Related-party transactions
- Amendments affecting economics or control
