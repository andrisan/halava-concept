# Halava Founder Documents

> Last updated: March 9, 2026
> Status: Working founder package

---

## Overview

This folder contains the working document set for the Halava founder structure.

Current working assumptions:

- Halava legal form is open and will be finalized as either `Kabushiki-Kaisha (KK)` or `Godo-Kaisha (GK)`.
- Target ownership after full earn-in is `Founder 55%` and `Co-founder 45%`.
- Ownership is earned by milestone completion and staged capital commitment, not by valuation.
- All earned equity remains subject to `4-year vesting` with a `1-year cliff`.
- Founder will work part-time until graduation, then switch to employee visa status in Halava for full-time work.
- `55/45` is fully earned only after both founders complete and sign off Stage `3`.
- Stage `1` runway is planned for March 2026 through August 2026, with founder-first execution and additional part-time support added when founder capacity is no longer sufficient, after mutual discussion and with the decision recorded in writing.
- Founder Stage `1` part-time salary is fixed at `JPY 1,500/hour` and may be reviewed for adjustment only at Stage `2` by both founders' written approval.
