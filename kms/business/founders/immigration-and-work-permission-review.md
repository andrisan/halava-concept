## 1. Purpose

This checklist is for confirming when and how the Founder may legally contribute to Halava, receive compensation, and hold a management role in Japan, with the working assumption that the Founder works part-time until graduation and then transitions to full-time employee status in Halava.

---

## 2. Working Rule

- Part-time Founder work before graduation must remain within the Founder's permitted status and any required permission.
- No paid work should be performed for Halava unless the Founder's current status or separate permission clearly allows it.
- No business-management activity should be performed outside the Founder's permitted status.
- The switch to full-time employment in Halava should occur only after graduation and the required approval for a visa-status change.
- The CEO transition cannot be completed until work-permission and status questions are cleared.

---

## 3. Questions to Confirm with Counsel or Specialist

- What is the Founder's current residence status and permitted activity scope?
- Is part-time paid work for Halava allowed under the current status?
- Is separate permission required before any paid side work?
- Can the Founder hold management authority under the selected legal form, such as `Director`/`Representative Director` (`KK`) or `Member`/`Managing Member`/`Representative Member` (`GK`)?
- What is the cleanest path to full-time work for Halava?
- What is the cleanest path and timing to switch from student status to employee status in Halava after graduation?
- Does Halava need additional office, capital, or staffing conditions before a full management role is possible?
- What tax, pension, and insurance consequences start once the Founder begins paid work?

---

## 4. Documents to Prepare

- current residence card details
- current employment or visa-related restrictions, if any
- Halava founder structure summary
- planned role description for the Founder
- planned compensation start timing
- Halava incorporation documents will once be available

---

## 5. Trigger Points Requiring Review

- before any paid part-time work
- before graduation
- before filing any visa-status change for employee work in Halava
- before the Founder accepts a formal management role
- before the Founder becomes full-time CEO
- before founder compensation starts
