## SKU Definition

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Billing unit** | Per placement booking (one product × one placement type × duration) |
| **Pricing model** | Fixed fee per booking, tiered volume discount on monthly booking count (marginal) |
| **Applies to** | Merchants who purchase boosted visibility for their product listings |
| **Free tier** | None — every booking is charged |

---

## What is a Featured Listing

A Featured Listing promotes a specific product above organic results in category search or in the homepage carousel. The product appears with a "Featured" badge. Featured Listings are purchased visibility slots and do not affect a product's organic search ranking.

## Tiered Volume Discount

The per-booking rate decreases as monthly booking count increases. Tiers are measured by the number of distinct bookings purchased in a calendar month, applied on a **marginal basis** across all placement types.

| Monthly bookings | Discount | Effective rates: category 1d / 7d | Homepage 1d / 7d |
|------------------|----------|-----------------------------------|------------------|
| 1 – 3 | 0% | ¥500 / ¥3,000 | ¥2,000 / ¥12,000 |
| 4 – 10 | 10% off | ¥450 / ¥2,700 | ¥1,800 / ¥10,800 |
| 11+ | 20% off | ¥400 / ¥2,400 | ¥1,600 / ¥9,600 |

**Example:** A merchant who buys 12 bookings in a month pays:
- Bookings 1–3: standard rate
- Bookings 4–10: 10% off each
- Bookings 11–12: 20% off each

One booking = one product in one placement for a chosen duration. A 7-day slot counts as one booking (not seven).

## Pricing Rationale

### No free tier

Featured Listings are a paid marketing product, not a core operational feature. Every booking delivers a concrete, measurable placement slot. There is no operational reason to offer a free allocation.

### Volume discount for frequent advertisers

Merchants running ongoing promotional campaigns — seasonal sellers, importers with rotating product launches — benefit from bulk discounts that reward consistent advertising investment. The marginal discount structure means a merchant never pays a higher per-booking rate by purchasing more.

### ¥500/day category search in context

At ~200 impressions per day within a targeted halal category, ¥500 represents **¥2.50 per impression** — competitive with digital ad platforms for a high-intent niche audience. A halal-conscious consumer browsing category search is already in purchase mode.

---

## Booking Rules

- One product per booking — each slot covers one listing in one placement
- Multiple products require separate bookings
- Bookings are **pre-paid** at time of purchase
- **Partial refund** on cancellation: unused days refunded pro-rated
- Booking begins immediately on purchase unless a future start date is specified
