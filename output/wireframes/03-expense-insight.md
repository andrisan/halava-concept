## Implementation Notes

### Key Components Needed

- `ExpenseOverview` - Monthly total, budget progress
- `TransactionList` - Grouped by date with category filters
- `TransactionCard` - Merchant, date, amount, category
- `TransactionDetail` - Full receipt with itemized breakdown
- `InsightsDashboard` - Charts and statistics
- `WeeklyBarChart` - Weekly spending visualization
- `CategoryPieChart` - Category breakdown
- `TopMerchantsList` - Ranked merchant spending
- `PriceHistoryCard` - Item purchase history
- `FrequentItemCard` - Frequently bought item
- `ProductHistoryBadge` - Inline history on product page
- `MyQRCode` - Dynamic QR with timer

### API Dependencies

- `GET /api/expenses` - Purchase history
- `GET /api/expenses/{id}` - Transaction detail
- `GET /api/expenses/insights` - Spending analytics
- `GET /api/expenses/prices/{productId}` - Personal price history
- `GET /api/expenses/frequent` - Frequently bought items
- `GET /api/user/qr` - Generate QR code for linking

### Design Tokens Required

- Colors: Category colors (groceries, dining, shopping)
- Charts: Bar chart, pie chart color palette
- Typography: Currency formatting, percentage
- Progress: Budget progress bar

### Integration Points

- **POS Integration:** In-store purchases linked via QR scan
- **Product Pages:** Show personal purchase history
- **Order History:** Unified with expense tracking
- **Export:** CSV/PDF export for personal records
