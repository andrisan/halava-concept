## Overview

Inventory Management enables merchants to track stock levels, set low stock thresholds, perform stocktakes, and receive alerts. Stock automatically decreases with sales (both online and POS) and can be manually adjusted for received shipments or damaged goods.

## Implementation Notes

### Key Components Needed

- `InventoryOverview` - Summary stats and product table
- `StockSummaryCards` - Total, In Stock, Low Stock, Out of Stock
- `InventoryTable` - Sortable product stock list
- `StockStatusBadge` - In Stock, Low Stock, Out of Stock indicators
- `ProductStockDetail` - Single product stock view
- `StockHistoryList` - Chronological stock changes
- `StockAdjustmentModal` - Add/Remove/Set stock
- `StocktakeMode` - Full inventory counting mode
- `StocktakeProgressBar` - Progress indicator
- `StocktakeProductCard` - Expected vs actual input
- `LowStockAlertsList` - Products needing attention

### API Dependencies

- `GET /api/inventory` - List all products with stock
- `GET /api/inventory/{id}` - Product stock details
- `GET /api/inventory/{id}/history` - Stock change history
- `POST /api/inventory/{id}/adjust` - Adjust stock
- `GET /api/inventory/alerts` - Low stock alerts
- `POST /api/inventory/stocktake/start` - Begin stocktake
- `POST /api/inventory/stocktake/count` - Submit count
- `POST /api/inventory/stocktake/complete` - Finalize stocktake

### Design Tokens Required

- Colors: In Stock (green), Low Stock (yellow/warning), Out of Stock (red)
- Typography: Stock numbers (large), SKU (monospace)
- Progress: Stocktake progress bar
- Tables: Sortable headers, row hover states

### Adjustment Types

| Type | Use Case | Effect |
|------|----------|--------|
| **Add Stock** | Shipment received | +N units |
| **Remove Stock** | Damaged, expired | -N units |
| **Set Exact** | Stocktake correction | Set to N |

### Automatic Stock Changes

Stock automatically decreases when:
- Online order is placed
- POS sale is completed

Stock changes are logged with:
- Timestamp
- Change amount (+/-)
- Source (order ID, POS transaction, manual)
- Reason (for manual adjustments)

### Alert Settings

- Enable/disable email notifications
- Set notification frequency (immediate, daily digest)
- Set custom thresholds per product
- Exclude specific products from alerts
