## Overview

The POS (Point of Sale) feature enables merchants to process in-store sales. It's desktop/tablet only and includes offline support, customer QR linking (for Expense Insight), and quota management. Advanced features like table management, split bills, and modifiers are available in v1.1.

## Implementation Notes

### Key Components Needed

- `POSLayout` - Split-screen product grid + cart
- `POSTopbar` - Status indicator, daily stats, menu
- `ProductGrid` - Touch-optimized product tiles
- `CategoryTabs` - Category filter buttons
- `POSCartPanel` - Current transaction items
- `CartItemRow` - Product with quantity controls
- `CheckoutModal` - Payment method, cash handling
- `ReceiptModal` - Post-sale options
- `PreparedOrdersQueue` - Online orders awaiting payment
- `QuotaBanner` - Usage meter with top-up CTA
- `TableGrid` (v1.1) - Visual table management
- `SplitBillModal` (v1.1) - Bill splitting options
- `ModifierModal` (v1.1) - Product customization

### API Dependencies

- `GET /api/pos/products` - Product catalog
- `GET /api/pos/categories` - Product categories
- `POST /api/pos/transactions` - Create transaction
- `GET /api/pos/transactions/today` - Today's sales
- `GET /api/pos/prepared-orders` - Orders awaiting payment
- `POST /api/pos/transactions/{id}/receipt` - Send receipt
- `GET /api/pos/quota` - Current quota usage
- `POST /api/pos/link-customer` - Link customer QR

### Design Tokens Required

- Colors: Online/offline status indicators
- Typography: Prices, totals (large, bold)
- Buttons: Large touch targets for POS
- Grid: Product tile sizing

### Offline Support

- **Local Storage:** Queue transactions when offline
- **Sync Queue:** Upload when connection restored
- **Conflict Resolution:** Server timestamp wins
- **Visual Indicator:** Clear online/offline status

### Quota System

| Plan | Transactions/Month |
|------|-------------------|
| Free | 300 |
| Growth | 3,000 |
| Pro | Unlimited |

- Top-up packs available for overage
- Visual progress bar in footer
- Warning at 80% usage
- Block at 100% (with top-up CTA)

### Customer Linking Flow

1. Cashier taps "Link Customer QR"
2. Camera activates to scan customer's QR
3. Customer shows QR from Halava app
4. Transaction linked to customer account
5. Appears in customer's Expense Insight
